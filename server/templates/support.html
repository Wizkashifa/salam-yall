Frequently Asked Questions

How are prayer times calculated?

Prayer times are calculated using the Adhan library based on your device's GPS location. The default calculation method is ISNA (Islamic Society of North America), but you can change this in the app's settings drawer under "Calculation Method."

How do I submit my business to the directory?

Go to the Directory tab and tap the "+" button in the top right corner. Fill out the submission form with your business details. All submissions are reviewed before being published to ensure quality.

How long does business review take?

Most business submissions are reviewed within 24-48 hours. Once approved, your business will appear in the directory with Google Places data (rating, photo, hours) automatically populated.

How do I get my event listed?

Events are pulled from the community Google Calendar. If you are a masjid or community organization in the Triangle NC area and would like your events listed, please contact us at admin@ummahconnect.app.

Why does the app need my location?

Location is used to calculate accurate prayer times for your position, determine the Qibla direction, show nearby masjids with distances, and sort restaurants and businesses by proximity. Your location data stays on your device and is never sent to our servers.

How do I enable/disable notifications?

Open the settings drawer from the Prayer tab (hamburger menu in the top left) and toggle "Adhan Notifications." You can also manage notification permissions through your device's system settings.

Where does the halal restaurant data come from?

Our halal restaurant directory includes 317 restaurants sourced from HalalEatsNC, a community-verified database of halal dining options in the Triangle area of North Carolina. Ratings are from Google Places.

Can I request my data be deleted?

Yes. Email us at admin@ummahconnect.app and we will delete any personal data we hold, including business submissions and push notification tokens. See our Privacy Policy for more details.

About Ummah Connect

Ummah Connect is a community app built for the Muslim community in the Triangle area of North Carolina (Raleigh, Durham, Chapel Hill, Cary, and surrounding areas). It provides prayer times, a halal restaurant directory, community events, and a Muslim business directory — all in one place.

This app is a community project and is provided free of charge.
